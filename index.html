THE ANTIMICROBIAL PEPTIDE REPOSITORY

Welcome! AMP-R, a repository of Antimicrobial Peptides (AMPs) consists of a vast library of 5544 structural AMP models. This entire dataset includes search enabled, easily accessible and downloadable FASTA files, PDB structures and their pictorial representation in 3D space, that can be used as predictive bioinformatics tools to study AMPs. It also includes a library of 2547 filtered peptides exibiting anti-Gram positive activity, along with results and resources from protein-peptide docking studies. AMP-R is an effective strategic tool to be used as a virtual screening approach to study and deal with antimicrobial peptides against multidrug resistant, biofilm-forming pathogens. This user-friendly repository can serve as a resource for future bioinformatics studies examining AMPs for antimicrobial and biological applications.
