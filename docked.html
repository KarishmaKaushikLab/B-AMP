ABOUT THIS SECTION

This section consists of a library of 100 AMPs that have been evaluated for their ability to interact with the active site triad (Cys-His-Arg) of the mutated Class C Sortase protein (in semi-open conformation) of Corynebacterium striatum. Based on docking results, these AMPs have been grouped into 11 categories based on a scale of 0-10. This preference score is based on interactions with the active site residues, other important protein residues, as well as docking scores. This is relevant in the context of selecting candidate AMPs for future in vitro and in vivo studies that target sortase enzyme related processes. Each card for a particular AMP contains its own input and output PDBQT files, model image and bond info, which can be downloaded by clicking on the respective icons.

The Standard tab includes docking analysis of the LPMTG Motif of Pilin Subunit of Corynebacterium striatum.
