ABOUT THIS SECTION

This section consists of a library of 100 AMPs that have docked on, or have interacted with the active site triad (Cys-His-Arg) of the mutated Class C Sortase protein in semi-open conformation of Corynebacterium striatum. For convenience, these 100 docking studies have been grouped into 11 categories based on a preference score on a scale of 0-10. A higher preference score denotes better interactions with the active site triad and a good docking score while lower preference scores denote lesser interactions and no docking with active site triad residues.

You can browse through sections arranged from 10-0 on the basis of priority (preference score 10 = high priority, preference score 0 = low priority) to be given while studying docking interactions for AMPs. Each card for a particular AMP contains its own input and output PDBQT files, model image and bond info, which can be downloaded by clicking on the respective icons.

The Standard tab includes docking analysis of the LPMTG Motif of Pilin Subunit of Corynebacterium striatum for comparison purposes.
